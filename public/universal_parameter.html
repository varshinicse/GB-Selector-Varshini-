Conflict Validation Policy
Engineering conflict checks protect against unrealistic user specifications (e.g. invalid speed ratios or torque/power calculations). Inconsistent parameters raise validation markers:
✓ Deviation < 2%: Warning only. Log discrepancy and proceed.
⚠ Deviation 2% - 10%: Design Error. Raise critical audit warning.
✗ Deviation > 10%: Hard Conflict. Throw validation block.
